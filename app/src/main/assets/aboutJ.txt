Mission Statement

Johnnie Java offers the student community of the College of Saint Benedict and Saint John's University (CSB/SJU) with a social environment that will foster academia along with the sale of specialty coffee. Johnnie Java is dedicated to enhancing the culture and academic-ethos of the college experience and will provide excellent customer service.

About Johnnie Java

Johnnie Java is a student-created and student-run organization. It is affiliated with the Donald McNeely Center for Entrepreneurship. Johnnie Java first opened in the Spring Semester of 2009 and is continuing to develop and benefit the students and the environment of CSB/SJU.
